How Listings Will Work

Edunancial will maintain a bilingual, searchable vendor directory. Members will be able to filter by:

  • Service type (legal, tax, marketing, etc.)
  • State / country
  • Language (English, Spanish, and more)
  • Business size or stage served

Vendor Partners will have profiles that clearly explain what they do, who they serve, and how Edunancial members can contact them.
