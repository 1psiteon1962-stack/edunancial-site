About Edunancial

We teach money the simple, practical way—through habits, real examples, and small wins that add up.

Our story

Edunancial started with a straightforward goal: help teens and families build strong financial habits early, using real-life situations instead of theory.

Who the student is (age 15)

  • Saves a fixed percentage of every dollar he earns.
  • Logs expenses and monthly goals with a notebook and an app.
  • Earns money through small jobs and local projects.
  • Buys physical silver and gold with his own funds.

This is financial education, not investment advice. Decisions should be made with a responsible adult.

Our approach

  • Clarity: plain language and short lessons.
  • Practice: small, repeatable actions over time.
  • Responsibility: safe use of money, respect for work, and goal setting.
  • Progress: basics → intermediate → advanced decision-making.

Mission

Build judgment, responsibility, and confidence so young people can make good decisions with money—now and later.
