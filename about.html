Our Story

Edunancial started with a simple, powerful moment. My son was 11 years old when he walked into my office just as a payment hit my phone. He asked, “How did you do that?” I told him: “I work with my head. Do you want to work with your head too?” He said yes—he just didn’t know how yet.

Today at 15, he can pick stocks with an 8–12% ROI, buys physical gold and silver, wants to learn options, and is planning a business. That growth proves what we believe: practical financial education helps people improve their own circumstances.

What We Teach

  • Red — Real Estate: property basics, financing, cashflow.
  • White — Paper Assets: stocks, options foundations, disciplined strategies.
  • Blue — Business: planning, operations, and execution.

Our goal is simple: clear, useful guidance that families and youth can apply immediately.
