Our Commitment

Edunancial, Inc. is committed to making our website and online content accessible to as many people as possible, including individuals with disabilities. We aim to follow widely recognized accessibility standards such as the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).

Measures We Take

  • Using clear headings and structured HTML where possible;
  • Providing text alternatives for key images and graphics;
  • Using readable fonts and sufficient color contrast;
  • Designing layouts with keyboard navigation in mind.

Limitations

Although we strive to make all pages accessible, some legacy content, third-party tools, embedded videos, or downloads may not yet fully meet all accessibility standards. We continually work to improve these areas.

Feedback

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our Site or content, please let us know so we can address the issue.
