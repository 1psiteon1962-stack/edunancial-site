# EDUNANCIAL COURSE CONTENT STANDARD

This document is the mandatory content standard for every educational course developed for Edunancial, regardless of who creates it (Claude, ChatGPT, human author, or future AI systems).

This applies immediately to the North American website and shall automatically become the standard for every future regional website worldwide.

## Mission

Every course must create genuine educational value.

Edunancial does not exist to sell courses.

Edunancial exists to help people build practical financial literacy and financial competency that they can apply in their own lives.

Courses must emphasize education, critical thinking, practical application, and informed decision-making.

No course shall promise wealth, profits, financial success, investment returns, or guaranteed outcomes.

## Standard Course Structure

Every course shall follow the same educational structure.

1. **Course Title**
2. **Color Category**
   - RED (Real Estate)
   - WHITE (Paper Assets)
   - BLUE (Business)
   - Future colors shall inherit this same format automatically.
3. **Course Level**
   - Level 1
   - Level 2
   - Level 3
   - Level 4
   - Level 5
4. **Learning Objectives**

   Students should clearly understand:
   - vocabulary
   - concepts
   - risks
   - opportunities
   - decision-making framework
   - practical application

5. **Estimated Completion Time**
6. **Required Knowledge**
7. **Course Introduction**

   Explain why the topic matters.

   Never begin by selling.

   Always begin by educating.

8. **Core Lessons**

   Each lesson shall include:
   - explanation
   - examples
   - practical application
   - common mistakes
   - important warnings
   - review summary

9. **Action Items**

   Every lesson should encourage practical implementation.

10. **Knowledge Check**
   - Quiz
   - Scenario
   - Case Study
   - Discussion Questions

11. **Course Summary**
12. **Additional Resources**
   - Books
   - Articles
   - Government resources
   - Official websites
   - Recommended videos

## Video Standard

Every course shall support:

- Short educational videos

Each lesson may contain:

- YouTube embedded video
- Transcript
- Downloadable PDF
- Audio version
- Reference links

Videos shall remain separate from the written lesson so they can be updated independently.

## Global Translation

Every course must support multilingual publication.

The content architecture must separate:

- Original English
- Translated text
- Video subtitles
- Voice narration

No language may require rebuilding the course.

## Course Consistency

Every course shall use consistent:

- Typography
- Icons
- Navigation
- Lesson order
- Progress tracking
- Certificates
- Downloads

## Red / White / Blue Standards

### RED

Real Estate

### WHITE

Paper Assets

### BLUE

Business

Each color must maintain the same educational structure while allowing different subject matter.

Future educational categories shall inherit this exact framework automatically.

## Legal Standard

Every course shall clearly state:

- Educational purposes only.
- Not legal advice.
- Not tax advice.
- Not investment advice.
- Not financial advice.
- Users remain responsible for their own decisions.

## Content Quality Standard

Content must prioritize:

- Accuracy
- Practicality
- Clarity
- Long-term usefulness
- Critical thinking
- Decision-making
- Financial competency
- Real-world application

Avoid:

- Fluff
- Marketing language
- Unsubstantiated claims
- Clickbait
- Fear-based selling
- Promises of wealth

## Edunancial Philosophy

Financial literacy provides knowledge.

Financial competency develops judgment.

Knowledge without action changes very little.

Competency is built through disciplined practice, informed decisions, and continual learning.

Our purpose is to help people build the financial competency that can create greater financial independence and greater opportunity to improve their financial circumstances for themselves, their families, and future generations.

This standard becomes mandatory for all current and future Edunancial educational content.
