# Global Content Governance Standard for All Edunancial Websites

**Priority:** Critical

## Applies To

- North American website (effective immediately)
- Latin America
- Caribbean
- Europe
- Africa
- Asia
- Middle East
- All future Edunancial regional websites

## Purpose

This document establishes the mandatory global standard for creating, reviewing, publishing, maintaining, and updating all educational content throughout the Edunancial ecosystem.

The objective is to ensure that every lesson delivers practical value, maintains high quality, and reflects the Edunancial philosophy of turning financial literacy into financial competency.

## The Edunancial Philosophy

Every lesson should help members:

- Understand a concept.
- Think critically.
- Apply what they have learned.
- Improve their decision-making.
- Build financial competency through disciplined action.

Edunancial does not promise wealth or financial success.

Instead, Edunancial equips members with practical knowledge, critical thinking skills, and financial competency that can help them make more informed financial and business decisions.

Substance always takes priority over marketing.

Practical education always takes priority over entertainment.

## Required Course Structure

Every course shall contain:

- Course Title
- Course Description
- Learning Objectives
- Estimated Completion Time
- Course Level
- RED, WHITE or BLUE designation
- Lesson Sequence
- Prerequisites

## Required Lesson Structure

Every lesson shall contain, where appropriate:

- Lesson Title
- Embedded Video
- Written Lesson Summary
- Key Definitions
- Important Concepts
- Practical Examples
- Reflection Questions
- Downloadable Workbook
- Checklists
- Templates
- Resource Links
- AI Coach
- Knowledge Check
- Quiz
- Recommended Next Lesson

## Video Standard

Educational videos should generally be concise and focused.

Preferred lesson length:

Approximately 3-10 minutes.

Longer subjects should be divided into multiple logical lessons.

Each lesson should teach one primary concept.

## Content Quality Standard

Every lesson must be:

- Accurate
- Practical
- Easy to understand
- Mobile friendly
- Professionally written
- Free of unnecessary jargon
- Consistent with the Edunancial teaching philosophy

## AI Coach Standard

Every lesson should include AI support that:

- Reinforces concepts
- Encourages critical thinking
- Asks thoughtful follow-up questions
- Suggests additional lessons
- Encourages practical application

The AI Coach shall never provide individualized legal, tax, financial, investment, medical, or other regulated professional advice.

## Global Language Standard

Every lesson shall support localization.

Languages may vary by region.

Translations should preserve meaning rather than perform literal word-for-word translation.

Examples should be localized whenever appropriate.

## Review Requirements

Before publication every lesson should be reviewed for:

- Technical accuracy
- Readability
- Grammar
- Spelling
- Formatting
- Mobile presentation
- Accessibility
- Regulatory compliance
- Internal consistency

## Compliance Standard

Educational content must avoid:

- Guaranteed outcomes
- Guaranteed investment returns
- Guaranteed business success
- Guaranteed financial independence
- Misleading claims
- Statements that could reasonably be interpreted as professional legal, tax, accounting, investment, or financial advice

Use educational language throughout.

## Version Control

Every lesson should maintain:

- Version Number
- Revision Date
- Revision History
- Author
- Reviewer
- Publication Status

## Content Management Portal

The Content Upload Portal shall support:

- Copy and paste from Claude or other approved authoring tools
- Embedded YouTube links
- PDF uploads
- Workbook uploads
- Image uploads
- Quiz creation
- AI prompt editing
- Draft saving
- Approval workflow
- Scheduled publishing
- Archive management

No coding shall be required for routine content publication.

## Global Marketplace Integration

Educational content shall integrate with:

- Books
- Courses
- Audiobooks
- Certifications
- AI Learning Tools
- Downloadable Resources
- Marketplace products

## Permanent Global Standard

This Content Governance Standard becomes mandatory for every Edunancial website, every course, every lesson, every language, every instructor, every AI-generated lesson, and every future educational product.

The goal is to build one of the world's most trusted libraries of practical financial education by emphasizing quality, consistency, critical thinking, and real-world application rather than quantity alone.
