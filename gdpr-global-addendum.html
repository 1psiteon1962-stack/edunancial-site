Global Privacy Addendum (GDPR / International)

Last Updated: ____________________

1. Scope

This Global Privacy Addendum supplements our main Privacy Policy for users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and other regions with similar data protection laws (“GDPR Regions”).

2. Legal Bases for Processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: To provide the Services you request;
  • Consent: For email marketing or certain cookies;
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our Services, prevent fraud, and protect our rights;
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws.

3. Data Subject Rights

If you are in a GDPR Region, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data;
  • Request correction or deletion;
  • Restrict or object to certain processing;
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format;
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent);
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

4. International Transfers

Edunancial is based in the United States. If you are in a GDPR Region, your data may be transferred to the U.S. or other countries that may not provide the same level of data protection. We implement appropriate safeguards, such as contractual clauses, to protect your information.
