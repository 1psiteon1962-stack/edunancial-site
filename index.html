Our Story

One afternoon when my son was 11, he walked in just as I finished a call with a business client. My phone lit up—an online deposit had arrived. His eyes got wide: “How did you make money like that?”

“By using my head,” I said. “Do you want to learn how?” “Yes,” he answered, “but I don’t know how.”

That moment planted the seed. By 15, he was buying physical silver and gold, analyzing companies, and practicing how to select and sell stocks—aiming for a steady 8–12% ROI.

That journey became Edunancial: practical financial literacy for anyone who wants to build a better future.
