Our Story

One afternoon when my son was 11, he walked in just as an online deposit arrived. His eyes got wide: “How did you make money like that?” I said, “By using my head.” Then I asked him, “Do you want to work with your head?” “Yes,” he answered, “but I don’t know how.” That’s when Edunancial was born.

By 15 he was buying physical silver and gold, analyzing companies, and practicing how to select and sell stocks—aiming for a steady, realistic 8–12% ROI. We learned together: simple frameworks, practical skills, and disciplined habits. Our mission is simple: help families build practical financial literacy—one page at a time.
