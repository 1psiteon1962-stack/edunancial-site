Our Story

When my 11-year-old son walked in as I ended a call with a client, my phone lit up with an online deposit. His eyes widened: “How did you make money like that?” I told him: “I used my head.” He said, “I want to do that, but I don’t know how.”

That moment sparked Edunancial — a simple promise: anyone can learn financial literacy and use knowledge to change their circumstances.

At 15, my son bought physical silver and gold, began analyzing companies, and learned to select stocks — earning 8–12% ROI while building confidence and discipline. If he can do it, you can too.

We teach in red, white, and blue tracks — from fast intro lessons to deeper, action-ready trainings. Start small, build strong habits, and grow.
