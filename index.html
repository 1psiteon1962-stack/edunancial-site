Our Story

How Edunancial began

My son was eleven. I had just hung up a business call when my phone lit up — an online deposit. His eyes went wide. “How did you make money like that?” he asked. “I used my head,” I said. “Would you like to do the same?” “Yes,” he replied, “but I don’t know how.”

That’s when Edunancial was born — teaching money with colors anyone can remember: Red for Real Estate, White for Paper Assets, Blue for Business. Short lessons. Clear steps. Real results.

From 11 to 15 — results

At fifteen, my son was buying physical silver and gold, analyzing stocks, and aiming for an 8–12% ROI. Not luck — literacy. Not theory — practice. The kind anyone can learn.

That’s the heart of Edunancial: hope with a plan.
