Our Story

One afternoon when my son was 11, he walked in just as an online deposit arrived. His eyes got wide: “How did you make money like that?” I said, “By using my head.” He wanted to learn — and that’s when Edunancial was born.

By 15 he was buying physical silver and gold, analyzing companies, and practicing how to select and sell stocks with a steady, realistic goal of 8–12% ROI. We learned together: simple frameworks, practical skills, and disciplined habits. Our mission is simple: help families build practical financial literacy — one page at a time.
