Our Story

One afternoon when my son was 11, he walked in just as an online deposit arrived. His eyes got wide: “How did you make money like that?” I said, “By using my head.” He wanted to learn—that’s when Edunancial was born.

By 15 he was buying physical silver and gold, analyzing companies, and practicing how to select and sell stocks aiming for a steady 8–12% ROI. Our mission is simple: help families build practical financial literacy—one page at a time.
