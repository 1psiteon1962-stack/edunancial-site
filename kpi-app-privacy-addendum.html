Edunancial KPI App – Privacy Addendum

Last Updated: ____________________

1. Overview

This KPI App Privacy Addendum (“Addendum”) supplements our main Privacy Policy and explains how Edunancial, Inc. (“Edunancial,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Edunancial KPI tools, dashboards, and related scoring features (collectively, the “KPI App”).

2. Data We Collect Through the KPI App

Depending on how you use the KPI App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account and profile data (name, email, login identifier);
  • Business profile data (business name, industry, approximate revenue range, number of employees, time in business);
  • Self-reported metrics (goals, sales ranges, debt ranges, budgeting habits, investment habits);
  • App usage data (pages viewed, scoring actions, completion dates).

3. How We Use KPI App Data

We use KPI App data to:

  • Generate personalized KPI scores, dashboards, and recommendations;
  • Track your learning progress over time;
  • Improve our tools, content, and user experience;
  • Provide aggregate, de-identified analytics about user performance;
  • Send educational follow-ups or reminders where legally permitted.

4. Profiling and Automated Scoring

The KPI App assigns scores based on formulas and rules developed by Edunancial. These scores are intended to highlight strengths, risks, and opportunities in your financial habits or business systems. KPI App scores:

  • Do not make legal or financial decisions on your behalf;
  • Are educational tools only, not credit scores or credit decisions;
  • Should not be relied upon as the sole basis for any major decision.

5. Data Retention

We retain KPI App data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or improve our tools. You may request deletion of your data as described in our main Privacy Policy and User Data Rights page.

6. Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your KPI App data. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us with hosting, analytics, or email communication, subject to strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.

7. Your Choices and Controls

You may choose not to use the KPI App at any time. If you do use it, you can:

  • Update your information in your account (where available);
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data;
  • Object to certain processing where applicable law provides that right.
