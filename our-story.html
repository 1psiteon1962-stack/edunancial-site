Our Story

Edunancial began when my 11-year-old son walked in as I hung up with a client. My phone lit up with an online deposit. With wide eyes he asked, “How did you make money like that?” I said, “I used my head.” He said he wanted to learn—but didn’t know how. That moment sparked Edunancial.

By 15, he was buying physical silver and gold, analyzing companies, and aiming for an 8–12% ROI. We turned those living-room lessons into practical trainings anyone can use to change their financial path.
