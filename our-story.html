Our Story

How Edunancial began

My son was eleven. I had just hung up from a business call when my phone lit up—an online deposit. His eyes went wide. “How did you make money like that?” he asked. “I used my head,” I said. “Would you like to do the same?” “Yes,” he replied, “but I don’t know how.”

That moment is when Edunancial was born. We made a simple promise: teach practical money skills in colors anyone can remember—Red for Real Estate, White for Paper Assets, and Blue for Business. Short lessons. Clear steps. Real results—so a kid with curiosity and a parent with purpose can see what’s possible, together.

From 11 to 15 — results

At fifteen, my son was buying physical silver and gold, picking and analyzing stocks, and aiming for an 8–12% ROI. Not luck—literacy. Not theory—practice. The kind anyone can learn.

That’s the heart of Edunancial: hope you can apply, skills you can use, and the belief that better choices lead to better circumstances.
