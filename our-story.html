Our Story

“How do I make money with my head?”

Edunancial was born the night my son asked that question. He was 11. I didn’t have a textbook answer—so we built one.

We teach families how to turn ideas, skills, and grit into income: how credit really works, how to price your time, and how to make your first $100 the smart way—not the hard way.

No jargon, no shame. Real steps you can take today.
Tools that stick. Checklists, playbooks, and clear examples.
For the long game. Habits that turn “someday” into results.

Why it matters

Money is emotional before it’s mathematical. We start with the human story—yours—then add the numbers so they finally make sense.

From a question to a habit

At 11, he asked the question that started Edunancial. By 15, he shows the habit we teach: saving and investing his own money in silver and gold, analyzing companies, choosing when to buy, hold, or sell stocks based on expected ROI, and actively planning a business.

That’s the point: make smart money moves a normal part of growing up—and give families the language and tools to do it together.
