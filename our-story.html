Our Story

Edunancial began with a simple question from my 11-year-old: “How did you make money like that?”

He walked in just as a client payment hit my phone. I told him the truth: by learning how money works and using my head. He asked me to teach him. We started with basics—budgeting, saving—and quickly moved into buying small amounts of silver and gold, reading company reports, and tracking stocks together.

By 15 he could explain P/E ratios and place practice trades. Our goal was never to “get rich quick” but to steadily aim for 8–12% ROI with common sense, patience, and risk management.

That journey became Edunancial: a practical path for families who want financial literacy they can actually use.
