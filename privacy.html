Global Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice explains how Edunancial (“we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use our websites, services, courses, and memberships.

Information We Collect

  • Account data (name, email, membership tier)
  • Payment confirmations from processors (e.g., PayPal) — we do not store full card numbers
  • Course and content activity (views, downloads, completion)
  • Support communications
  • Cookies and analytics (to operate and improve the site)

How We Use Information

  • Provide services, courses, and downloads
  • Send educational updates and member communications (with your consent)
  • Prevent fraud and enforce policies
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing

We share data with service providers who help us operate the site (e.g., hosting, analytics, email, payment processing). We do not sell personal information.

Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the services and for legitimate business or legal reasons.

Your Choices

  • Unsubscribe links are included in emails
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion by contacting support@edunancial.com
  • Cookie controls are available through your browser settings

Regional Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (e.g., GDPR/CCPA). We honor applicable rights requests.

Children

Our services are intended for individuals 13+ (or higher in certain regions) with parental consent where required.
