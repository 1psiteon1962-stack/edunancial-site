Refunds & Cancellations

  • Courses (live): Full refund if you cancel at least 48 hours before start time. Within 48 hours, we offer a one-time transfer to a future date.
  • Mini-courses (live): Full refund up to 24 hours before start time; otherwise one-time transfer.
  • Digital products (books/pamphlet): Non-refundable once delivered.
  • Reschedules: If we reschedule, you may accept the new date or request a full refund.
  • How to request: Email support@edunancial.com with your name, order email, and SKU.

All refunds are processed back to the original payment method.
