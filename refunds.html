Refunds / Reembolsos

Subscriptions

Monthly plans are billed in advance and are non-refundable for the current billing period. You can cancel anytime to stop future renewals. Annual plans can be cancelled; unused months are refunded pro-rata to the original payment method.

Mini Courses

Courses are refundable within 7 days if less than 25% of the content was viewed.
